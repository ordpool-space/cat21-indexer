Trade a cat

CAT-21 buy-offers are buyer-initiated, ord-style. The buyer constructs a half-signed PSBT that offers a price for the seller's cat; the seller countersigns input 0 and broadcasts. No intermediary, no marketplace listing, no escrow — share the offer any way you like.

Make an offer

You want to buy someone else's cat. Pick the cat, set a price, sign your funding inputs; share the half-signed PSBT with the seller.

Accept an offer

You've been offered Bitcoin for one of your cats. Paste the offer PSBT, review the price and recipient, countersign input 0 and broadcast.

Offers can be shared in the wild. A buy-offer PSBT reveals nothing that won't land on Bitcoin anyway once accepted, and it's only useful to a willing buyer (it requires their own funding inputs + signatures). Distribute by URL, QR, paste, Discord, gist, email — whichever's most ergonomic. No transport-layer secrecy needed.
